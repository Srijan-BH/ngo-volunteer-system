General Questions

It is a comprehensive digital platform that connects passionate volunteers with NGOs and community organizations. We simplify the process of finding, joining, and managing volunteer activities — from event discovery and registration to impact tracking and reporting.

Volunteering

Getting started is simple: 1) Create a free account using the Register button. 2) Complete your profile with your skills and interests. 3) Browse available events and click "Volunteer" on any that interest you. 4) Once approved, show up and make a difference!

Not at all! While some events may require specific skills (like teaching or medical knowledge), the majority of our events are open to everyone. Your willingness to help is the most important qualification. We also provide training for specialized tasks when needed.

Events

After logging in, go to the Events page where you can filter by location, category, date, and skill requirements. Our platform also recommends events based on your profile interests and past activities, making it easy to discover relevant opportunities.

Yes, you can withdraw from an event up to 24 hours before it starts without any penalty. Simply go to your profile, find the event under "My Events," and click "Withdraw." We do ask that you cancel as early as possible so the spot can be filled by another volunteer.

Account & Profile

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address and you'll receive a secure reset link within a few minutes. The link is valid for 15 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support.

Yes. Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and will remove all your data including volunteer history. If you'd prefer to keep your records, you can deactivate your account instead, which hides your profile but retains your data.

NGO Partners

We'd love to partner with you! Contact us through the Contact page with your organization details. Our partnerships team will review your application and get back to you within 3-5 business days. Once approved, you'll get a dedicated admin dashboard to create and manage events.

Partner NGOs receive access to a full admin dashboard including: event creation & management, volunteer approval workflows, attendance tracking, impact analytics & reports, volunteer communication tools, and downloadable data exports. Premium partners also get dedicated support and priority listing.

Technical
